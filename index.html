What is this?

An evening of free tech talks in Edinburgh brought to you by NCR Edinburgh.

Tell me more

In case you've missed it the way we write software is changing.

Functional programming and other ideas from academia have swept across our industry and are being absorbed into (or replacing) our tech stacks.

Start ups now run on Erlang and Haskell. Even Java has Lambdas and lazy streams.

For one evening only we've brought together some great speakers from industry and academia to talk about new ideas from this wave of change and the ones still to come.

Expect accessible talks on cutting edge ideas. Think StrangeLoop, only free, and shorter, and in Edinburgh.

Did we mention that there will be drinks and pizza?
